Our Story

Founded in 2006 in Surabaya, Inosoft began as a boutique development house with a vision to bridge local Indonesian talent with global technology standards. Over 18 years, we've evolved into a trusted offshore development center serving clients across 10+ countries.

Our journey from a small team to 50+ expert engineers reflects our commitment to growth, innovation, and delivering software that truly scales with our clients' ambitions.
